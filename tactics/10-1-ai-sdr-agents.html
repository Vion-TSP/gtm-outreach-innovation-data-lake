Tactic 10.1 - AI-Powered

AI SDR Agents

Deploy AI agents that research prospects, write personalised emails, send outreach, handle responses, and book meetings automatically. AI SDRs work around the clock executing high-volume outbound with human-level personalisation at machine scale.

Effort: Medium Cost: Medium-High ICP: Mid-Market and Enterprise Accounts
01

Why It Works

▼
The core insight: AI SDRs solve the personalisation-at-scale problem. Human SDRs cannot personalise 100 emails per day. Traditional automation cannot write messages that feel human. AI agents deliver both scale and quality, researching each prospect and crafting contextual outreach automatically.

The psychology behind AI SDR effectiveness

  • Personalisation creates reciprocity - Even AI-generated personalisation triggers psychological response. When someone demonstrates they researched you, you feel obligated to engage
  • Consistency and persistence - AI never gets tired, discouraged, or distracted. Executes perfect follow-up sequences without human inconsistency. This persistence compounds results
  • Speed to lead advantage - AI responds to intent signals within minutes not hours. First vendor to reach inbound lead or website visitor wins disproportionately
  • Quality filtering - AI can qualify and prioritise prospects before human involvement. Sales reps only talk to pre-qualified high-intent leads, improving efficiency

Why AI SDRs specifically

Unlike basic email automation, AI SDRs make decisions. They research prospects, adapt messaging based on context, handle objections, and qualify leads. This intelligence layer makes outreach feel human while operating at machine scale.

The compounding effect: AI SDRs get better over time through learning loops. Every response trains the model. Every successful conversation improves future messaging. Quality improves as volume increases.

Best Practices

▼

AI Training and Configuration - Teaching Your Brand Voice

▼

AI quality depends on configuration quality

Generic AI SDR setup produces generic results. Invest time training AI on your specific value proposition, ideal customer profile, and brand voice. Quality inputs create quality outputs.

Critical configuration elements

  • Brand voice guidelines - Provide examples of tone you want. Formal versus casual. Technical versus accessible. Confident versus humble. AI adapts to examples you give
  • Value proposition clarity - Explicitly define what you solve, for whom, and why you are different. AI cannot infer positioning. Must be explicitly configured
  • ICP definition - Detailed ideal customer profile including company size, industries, roles, pain points, and buying triggers. Helps AI qualify and prioritise prospects
  • Objection handling playbook - Common objections and your best responses. AI uses these to handle pushback conversationally without human intervention

Training data to provide

Training InputWhat to ProvideWhy It Matters
Best performing emails 5-10 highest-converting human-written emails from past campaigns AI learns structure, hooks, and positioning that actually work for your market. Replicates success patterns
Customer case studies Detailed customer stories with before-after and specific outcomes AI references relevant case studies in outreach. Social proof improves conversion. Provides concrete examples
Competitor positioning How you compare to alternatives and your differentiation AI handles competitive questions and positions you uniquely. Prevents generic category messaging
Failed message examples Low-performing emails and common mistakes to avoid AI learns what not to do. Avoids patterns that generate unsubscribes or spam complaints
Iteration mindset: Initial AI configuration is starting point not final state. Review messages weekly, provide feedback, refine prompts. AI quality improves through iterative refinement over weeks.

Quality Control and Oversight - Human in Loop

▼

Full automation requires earned trust

Do not enable complete automation on day one. Start with AI drafting messages for human review. Graduate to selective automation. Only full automation once AI consistently produces quality you would send yourself.

Phased automation rollout

  • Phase 1 - AI drafts, human sends - AI generates messages, human reviews and edits before sending. Catch errors and refine prompts based on output quality
  • Phase 2 - Automated sending, human monitors - AI sends automatically but human reviews dashboard daily. Spot-check random messages for quality. Intervene if drift occurs
  • Phase 3 - Full automation with alerts - AI runs autonomously. Alerts triggered for unusual patterns like spike in unsubscribes or response rate drop. Human investigates anomalies
  • Phase 4 - Scaled automation - AI fully trusted. Human reviews aggregate metrics weekly. Focus shifts from message quality to strategic optimisation

Quality metrics to monitor

MetricWarning ThresholdAction if Triggered
Unsubscribe rate Above 2% per campaign Pause automation. Review recent messages for spam triggers or poor targeting. Refine ICP filtering
Spam complaint rate Above 0.1% Immediate pause. Review deliverability and message content. May indicate fundamental messaging or targeting issue

Spot-check routine: Even in full automation, randomly review 10 AI-generated messages per week. Ensures quality maintains. Catch drift before it impacts results at scale.

Targeting and Segmentation - Feeding Quality Inputs

▼

AI amplifies targeting quality

AI SDRs execute at scale. Poor targeting multiplied by AI speed creates massive waste. Invest heavily in prospect list quality. AI personalisation cannot fix fundamental targeting mismatch.

List quality requirements

  • ICP fit verification - Every prospect should match ideal customer profile. Filter by company size, industry, geography, tech stack. Quality over quantity
  • Role and seniority accuracy - Messaging for VP differs from messaging for Manager. Ensure job titles are current and accurate. Stale data produces irrelevant outreach
  • Intent signal integration - Layer intent data onto prospect lists. Prioritise companies showing buying signals. AI reaches high-intent prospects first
  • Negative filtering - Remove existing customers, churned accounts, competitors, and recent unsubscribes. Clean lists prevent embarrassing mistakes at scale

Segmentation for personalisation depth

Tier 2 - Mid-market prospects Moderate personalisation. Industry and role-based hooks. Balanced volume and quality 500-1000 prospects. Good fit but not strategic accounts

List refresh cadence: Update prospect lists monthly minimum. Job changes, company acquisitions, and market shifts make lists stale quickly. Fresh data improves AI effectiveness dramatically.

Human Handoff Optimisation - Smooth Transition

▼

Critical moment is AI-to-human transition

AI can generate interest and book meeting. But sales rep must deliver on expectations AI set. Poor handoff destroys all AI-generated value. Optimise transition ruthlessly.

Handoff best practices

  • AI provides context to rep - Before meeting, rep receives summary of AI conversation, prospect pain points discussed, and expectations set. Rep prepared not blindsided
  • Consistent positioning - AI and human must tell same story. If AI promised X, rep must deliver X. Misalignment breaks trust immediately
  • Acknowledge AI role transparently - Rep can say "I saw you were chatting with our AI assistant about X. Let me dig deeper into that." Transparency prevents awkwardness
  • Quick response time - AI books meeting, human confirms within 2 hours. Delay between AI engagement and human follow-up kills momentum

Handoff quality signals

Rep training essential: Sales reps need training on handling AI-sourced leads. Different from self-sourced leads. Prospect already qualified and educated. Rep must advance not repeat.

Common Mistakes

▼

1. Enabling full automation without testing

Letting AI send 1000 emails day one without human review means errors and poor messaging get amplified massively before you catch them.
Start with AI drafting for human review. Test with 100-200 prospects. Refine prompts and configuration. Graduate to automation only after quality proven.

2. Poor initial AI configuration

Skipping brand voice training and value prop configuration produces generic AI messages that underperform. Garbage in, garbage out.
Invest 4-6 hours in detailed AI training. Provide examples, case studies, positioning, objections. Quality configuration creates quality output from day one.

3. Feeding AI low-quality prospect lists

AI personalisation cannot overcome poor targeting. Sending to wrong company sizes, industries, or roles wastes AI capability and damages brand.
Rigorously qualify prospect lists before AI touches them. ICP fit, accurate titles, intent signals. AI amplifies list quality good or bad.

4. No monitoring after automation enabled

AI can drift over time. Quality degrades slowly without oversight. By time you notice, damage done across thousands of prospects.
Weekly spot-checks of random messages. Monitor unsubscribe rates, response rates, spam complaints. Alert thresholds trigger immediate review.

5. Poor AI-to-human handoff

AI books meeting but rep unprepared. Prospect repeats same info. Rep contradicts what AI said. Meeting fails despite good AI work.
Provide reps with detailed AI conversation summary before meetings. Train reps on handling AI-sourced leads. Ensure positioning alignment.

6. Expecting immediate results

AI SDRs require tuning period. First 2-4 weeks are learning and optimisation. Judging too early leads to premature abandonment of channel.
Commit to 60-day test minimum. Expect refinement period. Measure improvement week-over-week not day one performance. AI improves with iteration.
