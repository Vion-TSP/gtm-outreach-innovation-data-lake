01

Why It Works

▼
The core insight: Spreading touchpoints across multiple channels feels helpful rather than spammy. A prospect who gets one email, one LinkedIn message, and one call feels engaged - not harassed.

The psychology behind multi-channel

  • Mere exposure effect - Seeing your name across channels builds familiarity and trust
  • Channel preference - Different buyers prefer different channels. Multi-channel meets them where they pay attention
  • Context switching - A LinkedIn message after an email creates a "surround sound" effect
  • Reduced spam fatigue - 3 emails in a week = annoying. 1 email + 1 LinkedIn + 1 call = thorough
